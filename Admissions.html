Welcome to the Energy & Nano Photovoltaics Lab at Sungkyunkwan University.

Our research aims to advance next-generation energy solutions through nanoscale engineering,
with a particular focus on high-efficiency solar cells, smart materials,and integrated photonic systems.

We are always looking for passionate students who are interested in cutting-edge research in nanoelectronics, photovoltaics, and smart sensors. Our lab provides a collaborative environment and diverse opportunities for graduate-level research.

| Eligibility Criteria

Master
Those who have obtained a bachelor's degree (expected) and have obtained English application qualifications

Master's and Doctor's Integrated Course
Those who have obtained a bachelor's degree (expected) and have obtained English application qualifications

Doctoral program
Those who have obtained an English application qualification as a master's degree (planned)
The master's and doctoral integrated course is a course that integrates master's degree and doctoral degree courses, and when the integrated course is suspended, a master's degree can be awarded according to the standards set by the school rules and regulations.

| Required Documents & Submission

Access the Sungkyunkwan University Admissions Office website and submit an admission application form, self-introduction, and research plan.
Submit the transcript of the school you are from, the English transcript, the oral subject application form (for master's and integrated master's and doctoral programs), and the application form for academic advisor nomination (for doctoral course) to the administrative office of the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering by visit or by registered mail.
