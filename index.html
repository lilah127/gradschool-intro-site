Welcome to the Energy & Nano Photovoltaics Lab at Sungkyunkwan University.

Our research aims to advance next-generation energy solutions through nanoscale engineering,
with a particular focus on high-efficiency solar cells, smart materials,and integrated photonic systems.

1. About the Lab

The Energy & Nano Photovoltaics Lab at Sungkyunkwan University’s Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering focuses on next-generation energy technologies. Our research explores high-efficiency solar cells, nanomaterials, and optoelectronic systems that address global energy challenges.

2. Research Focus

We specialize in perovskite photovoltaics, nano-device integration, and smart energy systems. Through experimental innovation and theoretical analysis, we aim to translate advanced materials and device research into practical energy solutions. Collaboration with international research networks plays a key role in our work.

3. Education & Career Path

Graduate students receive rigorous training through academic research, journal publications, and collaborative industry projects. Our alumni pursue careers in leading tech companies, national research institutes, and prestigious universities worldwide.
